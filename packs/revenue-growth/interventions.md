# Revenue Growth — intervention library

Sweep ALL of these in the portfolio step. Score each; recommend / defer-with-trigger / prune-
with-reason. Nothing disappears silently (critic G6). `Stage` = the constraint it acts on.
`Rung` = birth rung for its riskiest capability.

## Contact & follow-up (most agent-able stage in existence)

| Play | Stage | Feasibility | Rung |
|---|---|---|---|
| Speed-to-lead responder (reply < 5 min, route hot) | contact | agent-able | L1 |
| Lead nurture sequences (multi-touch, per-segment) | contact | agent-able | L1 |
| Abandoned checkout / registration recovery | contact/close | agent-able | L1 |
| Re-engagement of dormant list / past buyers | contact/repeat | agent-able | L1 |
| No-show and pre-event reminder machine | close | agent-able | L2 |

## Compounding edges (prefer plays that feed each other)

content engine → outbound personalization corpus → retargeting audiences ·
proof engine → every close-stage play · founder-brand → warm inbound → speed-to-lead ·
webinar recordings → repurposing engine → social + SEO.
